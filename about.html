About Me

An insight into my motivations and philosophies

What are you most passionate about in software development?

I'm most passionate about building high-impact applications that are both user-friendly and scalable. I love the challenge of designing complex systems and seeing them come to life, especially when they solve a real-world problem.

What kind of roles are you currently looking for?

I am actively seeking software engineering intern roles where I can contribute to a challenging project, learn from experienced mentors, and apply my skills in full-stack development. I'm particularly interested in companies working with modern web technologies like React, Node.js, and Kubernetes.

Outside of code, what are your interests?

When I'm not at my computer, I enjoy swimming, running, and biking as part of the UCSD Triathalon team. I also like to spend my time playing guitar and chess.
